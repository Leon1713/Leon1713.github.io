Pickup & Return Process

Experience a seamless rental process designed for your convenience. From pickup to return, we ensure every step is smooth and professional.

Vehicle Pickup Process

Our streamlined pickup process ensures you get on the road quickly and confidently

Step 1

Rent a car on website

Select a car from the website and reserve it with a valid credit or debit card.

Step 2

Vehicle Inspection

Together, we'll conduct a thorough inspection of your chosen vehicle. We'll document any existing conditions and explain all features.

Step 3

Keys & Departure

Receive your keys, emergency contact information, and 24/7 roadside assistance details. You're ready to enjoy your luxury experience!

Vehicle Return Process

Returning your vehicle is just as simple and efficient as the pickup process

Step 1

Return to Location

Bring the vehicle back to our office at the agreed time. Please ensure the fuel level matches the pickup condition.

Step 2

Final Inspection

Our team will inspect the vehicle for any new damage or issues and update your final bill. This typically takes 10-15 minutes for a thorough check.

Step 3

Complete Return

After the inspection is done, the final amount will be billed to your account automatically. check your account for more info.
